"Instructions for Clearing"

This story follows Tammy, a teenager grieving her absent father's death while living with relatives in a town shaken by the murder of a girl named Mary. As the town cuts down the woods where Mary died, Tammy's grief, fear, and dreams of Mary become tied to the destruction of the forest.

A satirical piece told through a series of workshop notes from a writer named Lucy, whose novel changes more and more after each round of feedback. The humor comes from how creative writing workshops can overwhelm writers with conflicting advice until the original story almost disappears.
